Eco-Friendly Cleaning Products

Switching to eco-friendly cleaning products reduces toxic chemicals in your home and the environment. Here’s a guide to choosing natural cleaners that are safe, effective, and biodegradable.

  • Choose Natural Ingredients: Vinegar, baking soda, and lemon are powerful and safe alternatives to chemical cleaners.

  • Avoid Single-Use Wipes:Opt for reusable cloths and microfiber towels that you can wash and reuse.

    .

  • Dilute Concentrates Properly:Eco-friendly concentrates save packaging and reduce waste—follow dilution instructions for maximum efficiency.

  • Look for Certifications:Choose products certified as eco-friendly by reliable organizations like Green Seal or the EPA's Safer Choice.

Reference:

  • Environmental Working Group (EWG) - Guide to Green Cleaning

  • EPA - Safer Choice Label for Cleaning Products

  • Green Seal - Certified Eco-Friendly Products

  • Sustainable Home Guide - Natural Cleaning Ingredients
