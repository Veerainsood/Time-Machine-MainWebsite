Grow a Small Garden

Creating a small garden promotes biodiversity and helps reduce carbon emissions. This guide provides easy steps to set up a home garden, whether in your backyard, balcony, or windowsill, encouraging you to grow herbs, flowers, and vegetables for a greener lifestyle.

  • Choose Native Plants: Native plants adapt better, require less water, and support local wildlife.

  • Compost Your Waste:Use food scraps to create compost, enriching your soil naturally and reducing waste.

    .

  • Practice Companion Planting: Plant compatible species together to enhance growth and deter pests naturally.

  • Water Smartly:Water early in the morning or late in the evening to reduce evaporation and conserve water.
