Opt for Natural Light

Utilizing natural light reduces energy consumption and creates a healthier indoor environment. Here are ideas to make the most of sunlight in your home, from placing mirrors to enhance brightness to positioning furniture strategically.

  • Use Light Colors on Walls and Furniture:Light-colored surfaces reflect sunlight, brightening the room without artificial lighting.

  • Keep Windows Clear:Regularly clean windows and remove obstructions to let in more sunlight.

    .

  • Install Skylights:Skylights can bring natural light into spaces that don't receive direct sunlight.

  • Arrange Mirrors Opposite Windows: Reflect light deeper into your space using mirrors for a naturally lit room.
