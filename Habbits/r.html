Practice the 5R's: Reduce, Reuse, Recycle, Repurpose, Refuse

The 5R’s offer a holistic approach to minimizing waste and conserving resources. This section provides actionable ideas for integrating the 5R’s into your daily routine.

  • Reduce What You Don’t Need:Avoid unnecessary purchases and cut back on single-use items.

  • Refuse Harmful Products:Say no to items that harm the environment, such as plastic bags and straws.

    .

  • Reuse and Repurpose Items:Find creative ways to reuse materials and give new life to old items.

  • Recycle Correctly:Learn your local recycling guidelines to ensure items are properly processed.
